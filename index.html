About TedxBellsTech

The very first TEDx BellsTech event was held on the May 12th of 2018 in bells university where we had a lot of notable speakers and big personalities, such as Denola Gray, Aramide Surumoh. The event proved a success and the students of bells university were greatly enriched. For the next coming TEDx event which we intend to hold on the 3rd of July would be themed ‘MOSAIC

Theme

Our theme for this year’s TEDx BellsTech would be called ‘MOSAIC ‘. Which entails that different piece of individuals carry different experiences and stories coming together under the umbrella of TED to create a masterpiece. We believe the theme will allow us diversify our speakers and help to greatly impact Bells University.
